I've added a [`/blogroll`](/blogroll) [slash page](https://slashpages.net/#blogroll) to my blog with the list of blogs I read. The feed URLs are automatically extracted from an OPML extract from my RSS reader of choice, [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com).

The OPML file is stored in the `data` directory of the Hugo project, and the list is rendered using a shortcode, `opmlblogroll`. Credit to [Publishing an OPML Blogroll With Hugo](https://darcynorman.net/2024/04/01/publishing-an-opml-blogroll-with-hugo/) for the inspiration!
