I've been accepted to read for a [Master of Philosophy in Advanced Computer Science](https://www.postgraduate.study.cam.ac.uk/courses/directory/cscsmpacs) at the University of Cambridge and will be joining [Corpus Christi College](https://www.corpus.cam.ac.uk) as a *corpuscle*! I've been wanting to return to study for a good while now and it's very exciting to see it materialise!

At all inflection points, it's nice to pause for a moment of reflection on the trajectory. I'm monumentally grateful to all those who believed in me and supported me throughout my undergraduate and in the application process.

In particular, I want to thank my indefatigable referees: [Dr Anna Leontjeva](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anna-leontjeva/), [Dr Shaanan Cohney](https://www.linkedin.com/in/shaananc/), and [Professor David Byrne](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dprbyrne/).

There are many more lecturers and tutors from the University of Melbourne who had an outsized impact on my education, and I'm deeply grateful for the good fortune I had to learn from them.

I'm especially indebted to the generosity of the [Cambridge Trust](https://www.cambridgetrust.org), [Cambridge Australia Scholarships](https://www.cambridgeaustralia.org.au), and Mr Charles Allen for the support of the Cambridge Australia Allen Scholarship.

I hope to focus on homomorphic encryption for privacy-preserving machine learning.
If you work in the field, or are just based in the area, I'd love to grab a coffee!
